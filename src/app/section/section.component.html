Scan2Deliver

STEP 1

Customer places order on web or app and QR code is generated

STEP 2

Courier scans QR code when parcel is picked up at customer location

STEP 3

QR code is used to identify and sort parcel at hub or branch

STEP 4

Courier scans QR code to make delivery

STEP 5

Recipient presents QR code as identification to take delivery from courier
